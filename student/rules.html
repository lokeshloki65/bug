Exam Rules & Instructions

● Secure Environment: The test will be conducted in a forced fullscreen mode. Escaping fullscreen is considered a violation.
● Anti-Cheating: Tab switching, window switching, or opening Developer Tools will trigger a warning.
● Restrictions: Right-click, Copy, Paste, and Text Selection are disabled within the exam environment.
● Malpractice: First violation results in a warning. Second violation will lead to **automatic submission** of the test.
● Evaluation: Your code will be evaluated strictly against the correct solution. You must fix all bugs before proceeding to the next node.
● Timer: You have 30 minutes for all questions. The test submits automatically when time expires.
